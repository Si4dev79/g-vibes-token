The Purpose of G-Vibes Token

G-Vibes Token is designed to solve the fundamental problems in today's creator economy:

Fair Compensation

Traditional platforms take 30-70% of creator revenue. G-Vibes ensures creators receive 90% of all revenue generated through their content, with transparent on-chain payments.

Content Ownership

Creators maintain full ownership of their content through NFT integration, allowing them to monetize across platforms while proving authenticity and preventing unauthorized use.

Algorithm Transparency

Our open-source recommendation algorithm eliminates black-box promotion, ensuring content reaches audiences based on genuine interest rather than paid promotion.

Censorship Resistance

Decentralized storage and distribution protect creators from arbitrary censorship while still maintaining community standards through governance voting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about G-Vibes Token

How do I participate in the presale?

+

To participate in the G-Vibes Token presale, you need to connect your wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.), complete KYC verification if required, and purchase tokens using ETH, BNB, USDT, or credit card. The minimum purchase amount is $100.

When will tokens be distributed?

+

Tokens will be distributed according to the vesting schedule: 20% at Token Generation Event (TGE) which will occur approximately 2 weeks after the presale ends, and the remaining 80% will be released linearly over 6 months.

What blockchain will G-Vibes use?

+

G-Vibes Token will be deployed on the Polygon blockchain. We chose Polygon for its low transaction fees, high throughput, and compatibility with Ethereum, making it ideal for our creator economy platform.

How will funds be utilized?

+

Funds raised during the presale will be allocated as follows: 40% for platform development, 25% for marketing and user acquisition, 20% for operations and team expansion, 10% for liquidity provision, and 5% for legal and compliance.

What makes G-Vibes different from other social tokens?

+

G-Vibes is specifically designed for the creator economy with features like direct creator support, transparent revenue sharing, community governance, and content ownership through NFTs. Our focus is on authentic content and fair compensation for creators.
